The Seed it Through initiative founded in 2026 aims to restore hope in underserved communities by planting and nurturing trees where they are needed most. Based in Ireland, the project focuses on revitalising urban and rural spaces by increasing native tree cover, strengthening biodiversity, and creating greener, healthier environments for future generations. Working in partnership with local communities, schools, and organisations across Ireland, Seed It Through transforms vacant land, schoolyards, and public spaces into thriving green areas. Through hands-on planting initiatives and community engagement , the initiative empowers people to take an active role in building a more sustainable and resilient future — one tree at a time.

Our Mission

Revitalising our Future for a Better Tomorrow

Seed It Through is not just about planting trees! it is about empowering communities to take control of their environment and future. By working side by side with local residents, the project helps people develop valuable skills in sustainability, teamwork, and long-term planning. Each tree planted becomes a symbol of growth, resilience, and shared responsibility.
