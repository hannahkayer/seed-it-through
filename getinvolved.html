How To Support Seed It Through

Seed It Through appreciates any contribution, big or small. There are many ways you can support Seed It Through and help us plant more trees worldwide:

  • Volunteer
  • Make a Donation
  • Partner With Us
  • Help Spread the Word

Volunteer

Help us plant trees in your local community or assist with events and outreach programs.

Sign Up to Volunteer

Donate

Every donation to Seed It Through is directed to our core mission of tree planting. Donations help us purchase saplings and tools, and support conservation management and restoration projects in local communities and around the world.

Make a Donation

Partner With Us

Help us care for our planet's forests, woodlands and hedgerows by becoming a business partner with Seed It Through. By sponsoring our tree-planting projects, your organisation can help rebuild forests and support greener communities around the world.

Become a Partner

Spread the Word

Support our mission by sharing our work across social media and following our partner organisation Home Tree pages. Raising awareness is just as important as donating and helps us reach more communities in need.
